GMO Crops - Good, Bad, or In Between?

Farmers have been able to dramatically increase their crop yields (quantity of the plants grown) because of Genetically Modified Organisms (GMO). Typically, the plants are genetically engineered to be either resistant to insect pests or to be resistant to herbicides (weed killing chemicals).
