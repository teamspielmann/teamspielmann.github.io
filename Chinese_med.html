Why does traditional Chinese medicine help to prevent malaria?

Youyou Tu became a Nobel Prize winner for conducting a scientific study on the traditional Chinese medicine's approach to preventing malaria. She found that the treatment approach contained Artemisinin which led to the creation of a new class of antimalarial drugs.
