Racism and the Plague in San Francisco

When the Plague came to SanFrancisco, scientists thought that it was predominantly spread from person to person. This caused racism against Asian Americans as it was assumed that it was coming from Chinese immigrants. Eventually 1 scientist discovered that the plague was actually spread by rats, however he had a hard time convincing leaders that he was right.
