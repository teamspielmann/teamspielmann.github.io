Thalidomide - the medication that caused birth defects

Between 1957 and 1962, more than 10,000 babies in Western Germanys were born with physical abnormalities caused by the drug thalidomide. This drug was prescirbed by doctors to thousands of women before it was accepted that it led to abnormalities.
